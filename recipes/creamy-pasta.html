Creamy White Pasta

Desciption

A simple, cream based pasta to which you can easily add your favourite meat, veggies, and cheeses. The secret is the fresh, homemade sauce, which you can mold to your liking.

The dish contains 2 servings.

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta of your choice (we use spaghetti, but your choice might change depending on the veg and meat you add)
  • 400 ml double cream
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 - 3 garlic cloves, finely diced
  • 1 white onion, diced
  • 2 portobello mushrooms, sliced thinly
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil
  • 1/2 to a 1 lemon, depending on how much acidity you would like
  • Cooking oil

Instructions

  1. Add the double cream to a pot on low heat with a pinch of salt. Do not bring the sauce to a boil.
  2. Add the garlic to some cooking oil in a pan over a medium heat.
  3. When the garlic becomes aromatic, add the diced onion. Sauté until translucent
  4. Add the mushroom, and lower to medium-low heat whilst continuing to sauté.
  5. Grate Parmesan cheese into the cream and stir. Taste regularly until satisfied.
  6. Cook the pasta in a pot with boiling water - add salt
  7. Add the pasta and sauce to the pan with the veg and mix well. Add a small amount of pasta water - don't worry about adding too much, you can always just let the mix reduce for a few minutes!

And done! Serve with some basil and sliced lemon to garnish - just like that, you've got yourself a delicious, fresh, homemade pasta dish!
